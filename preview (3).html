Smart Energy and Resilience Analytics for interconnected infrastructure systems.

The Smart Energy and Resilience Analytics (SERA) Lab at KFUPM advances data-driven, machine learning, and optimization-based solutions to improve the resilience, reliability, and sustainability of energy, water, transportation, logistics, and other networked infrastructure systems under cyber, climate, and operational uncertainties.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Hasan Masrur

Dr. Masrur is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM). He is also a Research Scholar at the Interdisciplinary Research Center for Smart Mobility and Logistics.

His work advances data-driven, machine learning, and optimization-based approaches for resilient, reliable, and sustainable interconnected energy and infrastructure systems. Research interests include optimization, resilience and techno-economics of power and energy systems, energy systems modeling, multi-energy systems and microgrids, data analytics for reliability and maintenance, AI/ML applications, and electric vehicle integration into the grid.
